RT240
The RT240 is the world’s only Rough Terrain Reach Stacker. It’s the only machine that can operate in virtually any terrain, in extreme weather and in up to 5 feet of water. The RT240 is built to work in the harshest conditions while safely handling up to 65,000 pounds
Military RTCH
The RT240, dubbed the RTCH, was created for the U.S. Army and its allies to assist with containerized loads where infrastructure had been destroyed or was nonexistent. With over 1,300 units in use, the RT240 has revolutionized logistics of supplying soldiers all over the world.
RT022
The RT022 has a 5,000 pound lifting capability and a fourteen inch ground clearance. Designed to load and unload containers in the harshest environments, this piece of equipment was designed for and proven by the U.S. Military.
